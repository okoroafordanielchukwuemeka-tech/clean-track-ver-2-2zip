# PART 1 — ORDER TIMER SYSTEM

## 1. Operational Countdown Timers

Every active order should display:

* time remaining
  OR
* overdue duration

Examples:

* 18h left
* 5h left
* Overdue by 3h

IMPORTANT:
This timer tracks operational completion only.

It is completely separate from:

* customer pickup timing
* partial pickup logic

---

## 2. Countdown Logic

Timers should be calculated from:

* order createdAt
* turnaround duration
* processingDueAt

Ensure calculations remain accurate after refresh/reload.

---

# PART 2 — LAUNDRY TURNAROUND SETTINGS

## 3. Laundry-Level SLA Settings

Owners should be able to configure:

* standard turnaround
* express turnaround
* premium turnaround

Examples:

* Standard → 48h
* Express → 24h
* Premium → 72h

These settings should automatically apply to new orders.

---

## 4. Service-Level Timing Support

Allow services to optionally override default turnaround settings.

Example:
Blanket Cleaning may require longer completion time.

---

# PART 3 — URGENCY SYSTEM

## 5. Due Soon Detection

Automatically classify:

* due within 24h
* due within 12h
* due within 5h

---

## 6. Overdue Detection

Automatically detect and flag:

* overdue operational orders

* alerts

* notification events

## 7. Urgency Color System

Create a clean operational color hierarchy:

Safe → green
Attention → amber

* orders table

* notification center

# PART 4 — OPERATIONAL PRIORITIZATION

## 8. Queue Prioritization

Automatically prioritize urgent orders.

Workers should immediately see:

* most urgent first
* overdue first
* due-soon next

without manually sorting.

---

## 9. Worker Operational Guidance

Worker Station should clearly separate:

* overdue orders
* urgent orders
* normal queue

Reduce worker confusion as much as possible.

---

## 10. Owner Operational Visibility

Dashboard should show:

* overdue counts
* due-soon counts
* operational bottlenecks
* average turnaround performance

Keep dashboard visually clean.
