# CleanTrack Phase 1 — Milestone 2

## Pickup Workflow Audit

IMPORTANT

Do NOT implement anything yet.

This is an audit only.

Read the complete pickup workflow before writing any code.

---

## OBJECTIVE

Determine whether the pickup workflow is production-ready.

Treat this as a commercial SaaS audit.

---

## Audit

### 1. Pickup lifecycle

Determine:

* How pickups are currently recorded.
* Full Pickup workflow.
* Partial Pickup workflow.
* Final Pickup workflow.

Document every state transition.

---

### 2. Order status transitions

Verify every possible status:

Pending

In Progress

Ready

Partially Picked Up

Completed

Cancelled

Delivered

Identify any missing states or invalid transitions.

---

### 3. Partial Pickup

Verify:

Can workers pick only selected items?

Can remaining items stay active?

Does the customer balance remain correct?

Does inventory remain correct?

Can multiple partial pickups occur?

Can the final pickup automatically complete the order?

---

### 4. Payment interaction

Verify:

Outstanding balance after partial pickup.

Partial payment before pickup.

Full payment after pickup.

No negative balances.

No duplicate payment records.

---

### 5. Receipt behaviour

Verify:

Original receipt.

Pickup receipt.

Final receipt.

Partial pickup records.

Customer history.

---

### 6. Worker permissions

Verify workers can:

Record pickups.

Record partial pickups.

Cannot edit historical pickups.

Cannot bypass completed orders.

Attempt direct API access.

Expected:

HTTP 403

or

HTTP 404.

---

### 7. Customer experience

Verify:

Customer clearly understands:

Items collected.

Items remaining.

Outstanding balance.

Pickup history.

---

### 8. Failure scenarios

Test:

Picking up already collected items.

Picking up cancelled items.

Picking up non-existent items.

Network interruption.

Double submission.

Concurrent workers.

---

## Deliverable

Provide:

1. Current workflow diagram.

2. Missing functionality.

3. UX issues.

4. Security issues.

5. Data integrity risks.

6. Recommended improvements.

7. Production Readiness Score.

Do NOT implement any changes until the audit is complete.
