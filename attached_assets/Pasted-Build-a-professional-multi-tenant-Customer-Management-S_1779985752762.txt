Build a professional multi-tenant Customer Management System for Clean Track.

IMPORTANT:
Clean Track is an operational B2B laundry SaaS platform.
Customers are NOT direct users of the software.
The customer system exists to help laundry owners manage customer relationships, balances, order history, and operational intelligence.

GOAL:
Transform customers from simple order entries into full business profiles with operational history and analytics.

REQUIREMENTS:

1. Multi-Tenant Customer Architecture
    Every customer must belong to:

* a specific laundry workspace
* isolated by laundry_id

IMPORTANT:
No cross-laundry customer visibility.

2. Professional Customer Profiles
    Each customer should support:

* full name
* phone number
* address
* notes
* created date
* last activity date

3. Automatic Customer Linking
    When new orders are created:

* existing customers should auto-match by phone number
* duplicate customer creation should be prevented where possible

4. Customer Business Metrics
    Track and display:

* total orders
* completed orders
* active orders
* total spending
* outstanding balances
* average order value
* total pickups remaining

5. Customer Order History
    Allow owners to view:

* complete order history
* payment history
* pickup history
* partially picked up orders
* unpaid balances
* active orders

6. Customer Insights
    Create operational indicators for:

* repeat customers
* VIP/high spending customers
* customers with unpaid balances
* inactive customers
* customers with remaining pickups

7. Customer Notes System
    Allow owners/workers to save:

* clothing preferences
* delivery instructions
* fabric warnings
* behavior notes
* operational remarks

8. Quick Operational Actions
    From customer profile:

* create new order
* view active orders
* view unpaid balances
* call customer
* copy phone number
* future-ready WhatsApp structure

9. SaaS UI Requirements
    Build:

* customer analytics cards
* customer table
* customer profile modal/drawer
* activity timeline
* mobile responsive layout
* operational badges and indicators

10. Mobile Optimization
    The customer system must work smoothly on phones for busy laundry operations.
11. Backend Architecture
    Build:

* scalable PostgreSQL customer structure
* protected tenant-aware APIs
* optimized customer lookup queries
* proper customer/order relationships

12. Business Intelligence Layer
    Design the customer structure to support future:

* WhatsApp messaging
* loyalty systems
* rewards systems
* marketing campaigns
* customer analytics
* customer segmentation

13. Operational Simplicity
    The experience should remain:

* fast
* clean
* simple
* practical for real laundries

14. Technical Audit
    After implementation, explain:

* how customer isolation works
* how duplicate prevention works
* how customer metrics are calculated
* how the structure supports future WhatsApp operations
* any future scaling recommendations

IMPORTANT:
Do NOT build customer-facing portals or customer accounts.
Customers interact with laundries through WhatsApp and real-world communication.
This is an internal business operations system only.
