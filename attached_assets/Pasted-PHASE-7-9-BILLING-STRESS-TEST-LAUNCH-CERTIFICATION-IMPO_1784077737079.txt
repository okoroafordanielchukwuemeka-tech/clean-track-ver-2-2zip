IMPORTANT

Do NOT implement new features.

Do NOT redesign the UI.

Do NOT improve styling.

This phase is verification only.

Your objective is to break the billing system and prove it is production-ready.

If a defect is discovered, fix only that defect and re-run the affected tests before continuing.
