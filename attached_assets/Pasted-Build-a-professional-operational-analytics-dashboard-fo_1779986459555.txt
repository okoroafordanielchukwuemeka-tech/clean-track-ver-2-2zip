Build a professional operational analytics dashboard for Clean Track.

IMPORTANT:
Clean Track is a B2B operational laundry SaaS platform.
The analytics system should help laundry owners understand business performance, customer behavior, worker productivity, revenue trends, and operational bottlenecks.

GOAL:
Transform Clean Track from an operational tool into a business intelligence platform for laundries.

REQUIREMENTS:

1. Business Overview Analytics
    Display:

* total revenue
* outstanding balances
* total orders
* active orders
* completed orders
* partial pickups
* average order value

2. Revenue Analytics
    Build charts/cards for:

* daily revenue
* weekly revenue
* monthly revenue
* payment trends
* unpaid balances
* revenue growth trends

3. Customer Analytics
    Display:

* repeat customers
* VIP customers
* inactive customers
* top spending customers
* customers with balances
* customer retention indicators

4. Worker Analytics
    Track:

* orders handled per worker
* completed orders
* active assignments
* pickup processing activity
* operational productivity

5. Operational Analytics
    Display:

* pending workload
* processing bottlenecks
* ready orders awaiting pickup
* partially picked up orders
* pickup completion trends

6. Dashboard Widgets
    Build:

* analytics cards
* charts
* trend indicators
* activity feeds
* operational alerts
* quick insight sections

7. Mobile Optimization
    Ensure analytics work beautifully on phones.
8. SaaS UI Quality
    The dashboard should feel:

* premium
* modern
* fast
* clean
* highly visual

9. Time Filtering
    Support:

* today
* 7 days
* 30 days
* custom ranges

10. Multi-Tenant Isolation
    All analytics must remain isolated per laundry workspace.
11. Backend Architecture
    Build:

* optimized analytics queries
* scalable aggregation logic
* tenant-aware analytics endpoints

12. Future-Ready Analytics
    Design the structure to support future:

* WhatsApp analytics
* marketing analytics
* subscription analytics
* branch analytics
* AI operational insights

13. Technical Audit
    After implementation, explain:

* how analytics are calculated
* any performance considerations
* future scaling recommendations
* any areas needing optimization later

IMPORTANT:
Focus on operational clarity and actionable business intelligence for real laundries.
