CLEANTRACK — FIRST 100 USERS CONVERSION READINESS IMPLEMENTATION

IMPORTANT:

Do NOT build Paystack.
Do NOT build WhatsApp integration yet.
Do NOT add unrelated features.

The goal of this phase is to optimize the first paying customer journey.

CleanTrack already has strong security, multi-tenant architecture, admin controls, activation tracking, and customer success systems.

Now improve the onboarding and trial experience so a new laundry owner can sign up, understand the value, reach their first order quickly, and be motivated to upgrade.

Before coding:

Perform a complete audit of:

1. Signup flow
2. First login experience
3. Trial experience
4. Dashboard experience
5. Pricing visibility
6. Billing upgrade journey
7. Customer creation flow
8. First order flow

Identify any friction points.

Then implement the following improvements.

---

## 1. Change Trial Experience To Growth Trial

Current problem:

New users are evaluating a limited Free plan.

This reduces conversion because users cannot experience the full value of CleanTrack.

Change the 14-day trial experience.

During trial:

Users should receive Growth-level features.

Trial should include:

* Analytics
* Reports
* Multiple workers
* Advanced dashboard features
* All important workflow features

Do NOT change paid plan limits.

After trial expires:

Move user to their selected paid plan or free limitations.

Verify:

Trial users can experience the complete value of CleanTrack.

---

## 2. Fix Trial Messaging

Current issue:

Users discover the trial after signup.

Improve signup page.

Add clear messaging:

"Start your 14-day free trial. No payment required."

Explain:

* What they get
* When trial ends
* How upgrading works

Make it Nigerian-market friendly.

Avoid complicated SaaS language.

---

## 3. Fix First Login Experience

Current issue:

Users land directly on dashboard and feel overwhelmed.

Create a simple welcome experience.

After signup show:

"Welcome to CleanTrack 👋"

Explain:

"You are ready to manage your laundry business."

Show first steps:

1. Review your services
2. Add your first customer
3. Create your first order
4. Record payment

Include:

[Start Setup]

Button should take them directly to the next action.

---

## 4. Fix Branch Limit Warning

Critical UX issue:

New accounts see:

"Plan limit reached"

immediately after signup because their default branch consumes the limit.

This should NOT happen during trial.

During trial:

Show:

"Your trial includes your first branch."

No red warning.

After trial:

Show upgrade message only when relevant.
