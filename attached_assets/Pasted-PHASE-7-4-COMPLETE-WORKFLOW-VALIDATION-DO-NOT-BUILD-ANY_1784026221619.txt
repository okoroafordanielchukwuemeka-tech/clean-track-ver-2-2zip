## Navigation Audit

For every page answer:

1. Why would the user visit this page?
2. What is the primary action?
3. Is that action immediately obvious?
4. Can the action be completed quickly?
5. Are there unnecessary buttons?
6. Are there duplicated actions?
7. Are important actions hidden?

Recommend simplifications only.
