Perform a Backup, Disaster Recovery, and Business Continuity Audit for CleanTrack.

Mission:

Determine whether CleanTrack can survive:

1. Database corruption
2. Accidental production deletion
3. Failed deployment
4. Bad migration
5. Server outage
6. Replit outage
7. Region outage
8. Human error
9. Ransomware-style data destruction
10. Multi-day service interruption

Do NOT modify code initially.
